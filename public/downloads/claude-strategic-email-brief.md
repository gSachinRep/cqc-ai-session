# Strategic Email Brief

Role:
Senior functional leader

Scenario:

- You are announcing an AI adoption pilot across three departments.
- You want support, not resistance.
- The note should sound confident, practical, and collaborative.

Audience:

- Leadership peers
- Functional heads

Constraints:

- 150 words maximum
- Clear call to action
- Avoid hype and technical jargon

Ask Claude for:

- one primary version
- two alternatives
- five subject lines
