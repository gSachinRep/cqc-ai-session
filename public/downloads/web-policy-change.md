# Policy Change Note

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

Policy summary:

- New approval is required for discounts above 12 percent.
- Contracts with data-hosting exceptions now require legal review before submission.
- Deal teams must include implementation timelines in all customer proposals.
- Any non-standard payment term beyond 60 days must be approved by finance.

Intent:

- Reduce commercial leakage.
- Prevent avoidable legal exceptions.
- Improve predictability during onboarding.
