# Market Update

Date: March 2026

- Two competitors have announced aggressive discounting in the enterprise mid-market segment.
- A key channel partner reported longer deal cycles in the last six weeks.
- Public commentary from analysts suggests clients are shifting spending toward projects with faster payback.
- Early feedback from three recent prospects indicates interest remains strong, but scrutiny on implementation timelines has increased.
- One competitor is promoting "fully managed onboarding" as a differentiator.

Questions leadership should consider:

- Is pricing the real issue, or is speed-to-value the bigger issue?
- Are we losing deals because of economics, positioning, or execution confidence?
- Should the response be commercial, operational, or both?
